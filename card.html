ROCK-PAPER-SCISSORS

Rock Paper Scissors is an instransitive hand game, usually palyed between 2 people, in which each player simultaneously forms on of the three shapes with an outstretched hand. The three shapes are Rock, Paper and Scissor.

2048

2048 is a single-player sliding title puzzle video game written by Italian web developer Gabriele Cirulli The objective is to slide numbered tiles on a grid to combine them to create a tile with the number 2048.

PING PONG

Ping Pong is a table-tennis themed arcade sports video game, first manufactured by Atari. It was one of the earliest arcade video games. The player controls a game paddle by moving it vertically across the left or right side of the screen.

DINO RUN

The Dinosaur Game also known as the Chrome Dino is a browser game developed by Google and built into the Google Chrome web browser. The player guides a pixelated Tyrannosaurus Rex across a side scrolling landscape, avoiding obstacles to achieve a higer score.

SLIDE

Enjoy a challenging slide puzzle game that tests your wit and spatial skills. Rearrange shuffled tiles to reveal a captivating image. This game offers endless entertainment and sharpens your problem-solving abilities.
